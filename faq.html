FAQ

Answers to common questions about Velocity Apex and our games.

Is it mobile-friendly?

Yes. Our games are fully responsive and optimized for both desktop and mobile devices, including tap-to-play controls.

How are scores saved?

Your high score and leaderboard entries are saved locally in your browser using localStorage. This means your scores are private to your device.

Can I reset the leaderboard?

In your browser's developer console, run localStorage.removeItem('ar_leaderboard') and refresh the page. To clear your high score, run localStorage.removeItem('ar_high').

What browsers are supported?

Our games are built with modern web standards and are supported on the latest versions of Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge.

Are the games free to play?

Yes, all games on Velocity Apex are completely free to play. We are committed to providing accessible entertainment without paywalls or pay-to-win mechanics.

How can I provide feedback?

We'd love to hear from you! Please use the contact form on our home page to send us your thoughts, suggestions, or bug reports.

What keys are supported?

Spacebar or the Up Arrow to jump. 'P' to pause/resume. 'R' to save your score and restart the game.

Is an account required?

No account is needed to play. We prioritize instant access, so you can start playing immediately.
